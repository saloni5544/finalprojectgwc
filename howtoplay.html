HOW TO PLAY

To play our game you will be directed to the website PythonAnywhere where we keep our game. After the page loads you will see all of our code, copy that code to your account and you will be given the option to run it. To begin playing the game, run it. Have fun!

This is a text adventure game that tells you about the world's problems and helps you navigate the daily lifestyle of the location you decide to chose. To play the game you will begin by choosing a country or city as your startng point. By playing the game you will be able to experience some of the hardships other people go through around the world. While this is a light hearted game do be aware it is a reality for some.
